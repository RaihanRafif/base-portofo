FAQ (because humans always ask)

Quick answers. No thesis.

Is it good for working?

The vibe is generally work-friendly. If you need absolute silence, come earlier in the morning.

Do you have outdoor seating?

Outdoor seating is commonly listed. Availability can change, so check on-site.

What should I order first?

Try a magic latte (or your espresso preference) and pair it with a pastry. For brunch, go with French toast or pancakes.
